I Like Pokémon!

Welcome to my Pokémon fan page!

Hi! I am Dawn, and I’ve loved Pokémon since I was a kid. I enjoy playing Pokémon games and watching the anime. This website is where I share some of my favorite things about Pokémon.


Pokémon is easy to love because it isn’t just a game series—it’s a global world of fun, creativity, and adventure built by The Pokémon Company. The video games offer exciting journeys through different regions, while titles like Pokémon Go turn the real world into your own catching adventure. The creature designs are another huge draw; each Pokémon combines creativity, personality, and charm in a way no other franchise matches.

Beyond gaming, Pokémon expands into many different hobbies and experiences. The Pokémon Trading Card Game brings strategy and collecting together, attracting both casual players and dedicated fans around the world. Even competitive players get their spotlight through events like the Pokémon World Championships, where top battlers compete in an exciting, friendly atmosphere.

Pokémon also shines in real-world experiences. Stores like Pokémon Center and themed spots such as the Pokémon Cafe let fans enjoy exclusive merchandise, food, and immersive environments. Plush toys—especially the iconic varieties released over the years—add a comforting and adorable way to connect with your favorite Pokémon.

All of this is supported by strong global marketing that uses animation, events, collaborations, social media, and community engagement to keep Pokémon constantly alive and relevant. Whether through games, cards, plushes, cafés, championships, or everyday adventures, Pokémon creates a world filled with imagination, discovery, and joy—making it something truly worth liking.

in the Pokémon community.
